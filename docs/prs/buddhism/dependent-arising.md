:::note[Useful Definitions]
:::

Dependent arising is the idea that everything depends on something else. There is a chain of cause and effect for everything. For example, for a tree to exist, it needs:

* Rain, which needs
  * Proper weather conditions to form
* Sunlight, which needs
  * The Sun, which needs:
    * The Universe
* Soil, which needs
  * Organic matter from past plants and animals, which needs
    * Food
* etc.

This means that everything is constantly changing, because the supporting conditions are always changing, which themselves have changing supporting conditions.  
Our distance from the Sun is constantly changing, changing lengths of days and the amount of light the tree receives. The rain changes during different seasons, etc.
